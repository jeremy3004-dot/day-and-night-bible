**Then test on device or simulator:**

1. **Scroll-driven header collapse (SCROLL-01):**
   - Open Bible reader in read mode (any chapter with text, e.g., John 1)
   - Slowly scroll down — the top glass chrome (back button, Listen/Read toggle, overflow) should fade and slide up smoothly
   - The hero title and section heading should also fade and collapse
   - Scroll back up — chrome and hero should reappear
   - Verify the animation is smooth (no jank/frame drops compared to before)

2. **Swipe left to next chapter (SWIPE-01):**
   - In read mode, swipe LEFT (finger moves right to left)
   - The visible content should shift slightly left during the swipe
   - On release past threshold, the next chapter should load
   - Verify the chapter actually changes (book/chapter label updates)

3. **Swipe right to previous chapter (SWIPE-02):**
   - In read mode, swipe RIGHT (finger moves left to right)
   - The previous chapter should load

4. **Swipe boundary (SWIPE-03):**
   - Navigate to Genesis 1 (first chapter) — swipe right should do nothing
   - Navigate to Revelation 22 (last chapter) — swipe left should do nothing

5. **Vertical scroll not eaten (gesture conflict):**
   - In read mode, scroll up and down normally
   - Verify vertical scrolling works as before — swipe should only trigger on clearly horizontal gestures

6. **Audio sync during swipe (AUDIO-01):**
   - Start playing a chapter (tap Listen, then switch back to Read)
   - While audio is playing, swipe to next chapter
   - Verify audio transfers to the new chapter (or continues as expected per the existing behavior)
   - Rapidly swipe 3-4 times — verify no audio stacking or crashes

7. **Follow Along modal (MODAL-01):**
   - In listen mode, tap "Show Text" to open Follow Along
   - Verify the modal slides up with a bouncy spring feel (not the old stock iOS slide)
   - Close the modal — verify it slides down smoothly
