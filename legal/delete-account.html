Delete Your Account

Last updated: January 25, 2025

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your Selah account and all associated data, you can do so easily within the app or by contacting us directly.

Important: This Action Cannot Be Undone

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your account and profile information
  • All reading progress and history
  • Bookmarks and highlights
  • App preferences and settings
  • All synced content across devices

Option 1: Delete Account in the App

The easiest way to delete your account is directly within the Selah app:

  1. Open the Selah app on your device
  2. Tap the "More" tab at the bottom of the screen
  3. Tap "Settings"
  4. Scroll down to the "Data" section
  5. Tap "Delete Account"
  6. Confirm your decision in the dialog that appears

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion manually, please send an email to:

Email: curryj@protonmail.com

Please include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Subject line: "Delete My Selah Account"

We will process your request and delete your account within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

What Happens After Deletion?

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will be signed out of the app on all devices
  • Your data will be permanently removed from our servers
  • You can continue using the app as a guest without an account
  • If you wish to sync your progress in the future, you can create a new account

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data after account deletion. All your information is permanently removed from our database immediately upon deletion.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact us at curryj@protonmail.com.
